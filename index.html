About Shreeja Biotech

Leading the Renewable Energy Revolution

Founded in Surat, Gujarat, Shreeja Biotech specializes in converting organic waste into clean, renewable Bio-CNG. With over a decade of experience, we've pioneered innovative technologies that transform agricultural, municipal, and industrial waste into valuable energy resources.

Our mission is to reduce India's carbon footprint while providing cost-effective energy solutions that benefit communities, industries, and the environment.

Our Mission

"To develop, innovate, and implement world-class renewable energy solutions that transform organic waste into high-value, sustainable energy resources while promoting environmental responsibility."

Our Vision

"To establish Shreeja Bio-Tech as a global leader in Waste-to-Energy and Bio-CNG production, delivering cost-effective, sustainable, and high-performance solutions that contribute to a cleaner, greener future."

Our Bio-CNG Production Process

1

Waste Collection

Organic waste is sourced from agricultural, municipal, and industrial partners across Gujarat.

2

Pre-Treatment

Waste undergoes segregation, shredding, and preparation for optimal digestion.

3

Anaerobic Digestion

Microorganisms break down organic matter in oxygen-free digesters, producing biogas.

4

Biogas Purification

Our hybrid systems remove impurities to achieve 98% methane purity for Bio-CNG.

5

Compression & Storage

Purified gas is compressed to 250 bar and stored in specialized cylinders.

6

Distribution

Bio-CNG is distributed to industrial clients, CNG stations, and commercial users.
