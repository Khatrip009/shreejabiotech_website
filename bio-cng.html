What is Bio-CNG?

Bio-CNG (Compressed Biogas) is a purified form of biogas that has been upgraded to remove impurities and compressed to a pressure similar to CNG. It contains over 95% methane and has calorific value comparable to fossil-based natural gas.

Why Choose Bio-CNG?

Bio-CNG is a sustainable alternative to fossil fuels that helps reduce greenhouse gas emissions while providing a reliable energy source. Our plants convert agricultural waste, municipal solid waste, and industrial effluents into clean energy.

Our Technology

Shreeja Biotech uses advanced anaerobic digestion technology combined with our patented semi-cryogenic purification system to produce Bio-CNG with 98% methane purity. Our systems are designed for maximum efficiency and minimal environmental impact.
